How do I choose the right size toy?

Always choose toys based on your pet's current size. A toy that is too small can be a choking hazard, while a toy too large might be difficult for them to carry.

Are "indestructible" toys actually indestructible?

No toy is truly indestructible. While we use heavy-duty materials, always supervise your pet during initial playtime.

How often should I clean my pet's toys?

Rubber toys should be washed weekly with mild soap. Plush toys can usually go in the washing machine on a delicate cycle.
