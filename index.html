Salon FAQ

Should I wash my hair before my color service?

We prefer you arrive with clean, dry hair washed the day before. Please avoid heavy root powders or "touch-up" sprays, as they can create a barrier against the hair color.

What is the difference between Highlights and Balayage?

Highlights usually go to the root for a more uniform look, while Balayage is hand-painted for a natural, sun-kissed gradient that grows out beautifully with less maintenance.

Do you offer bridal or group bookings?

Yes, we specialize in bridal party styling. We recommend booking at least 6 months in advance. Please use the chat assistant for availability and pricing.
